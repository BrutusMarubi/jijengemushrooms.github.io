About Mushroom Farming

There is a huge market for quality Mushrooms. In particular Gourment Oyster Mushroom is growing in popularity. Not only is the demand high but Oyster Mushrooms are easy to grow. So how can you become a profitable Mushroom Grower? How can you make the type of money you have always wanted? Here is how...

Start With Oyster

It takes about six weeks from start to harvest time and no full time committment is necessary. You can still have a full time job or other committments and be a successful Mushroom grower. If you have a few hours a week to spare, then you have enough time to grow Oyster Mushrooms for profit.

How much can you make?

Oyster Mushrooms are currently selling at kshs 600 per kg; a room 10 by 10 sq ft can produce an average 1000kg of fresh mushrooms every two to three months. That is around ksh 600,000 total sales average khs 150,000 sales a month. Need more money? expand the growing area to produce more mushrooms.

How to be a succesful Muushroom Grower

You need to set aside a growing space where you can control the temperature, humidity and amount of light. Mushrooms need varying amounts of all three at different stages in the growing cycle, and its vital that you can regulate them to ensure a good harvest. For a start, 10 by 10 sq ft room with Mud wall is just okay. Spend a little for roofing, 4 galvanized 3 metre iron sheets. You are ready to go.

How do i start?

Order ready to grow Oyster Mushroom Kit ready to fruit : 14 days to harvest guaranteed perfomance.
Get high quality substrate (Growing media with seeds spawn) and technical back up from us, call for details

Procedure 1

Maintain the temperatures in the growing room at the recommended temperature from the incubation period upto between four to six weeks.

Procedure 2

As the substrates start shooting, maintain right humidity, airflow, temperature and light. Mushrooms will then develop as you harvest on a daily basis. Harvest and supply your market.
